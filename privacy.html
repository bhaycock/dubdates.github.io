Introduction

Welcome to DubDates. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

Data We Collect

Calendar Data

  • When you choose to add events to your calendar, we request access to your device's calendar
  • We only add events you explicitly choose to add
  • We do not read, collect, or store any existing calendar information

App Usage Data

We store locally on your device:

  • Your favorite events
  • Events you've marked as booked
  • Events you're not interested in
  • Venue preferences
  • App settings and preferences

Technical Data

  • Basic device information for crash reporting
  • Performance data to improve app functionality
  • Internet connectivity status for event updates

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Show you relevant events and venues
  • Remember your preferences
  • Add events to your calendar when requested
  • Provide event recommendations
  • Improve app performance and functionality

Data Storage

  • All your preferences and selections are stored locally on your device
  • We do not store any personal data on external servers
  • Event data is cached locally for offline access

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Calendar API (when adding events to calendar)
  • Content delivery networks for event images
  • Device calendar services

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Choose what calendar events to add
  • Control app permissions through your device settings

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to secure your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Network communications use secure protocols
  • Calendar access is requested only when needed

Children's Privacy

DubDates does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes through the app.

Contact Us

App Permissions

DubDates requires the following permissions:

Calendar Access

  • Purpose: To add events you select to your calendar
  • When: Only when you choose to add an event
  • Control: Can be enabled/disabled in device settings

    • Internet Access

    • Purpose: To download event information and images
    • When: When the app is updating event data
    • Control: Can be restricted through device settings

    Your Choices

    You can control data collection and use by:

    • 1. Adjusting app permissions in your device settings
    • 2. Choosing what events to save or add to calendar
    • 3. Clearing app data through your device settings
    • 4. Uninstalling the app to remove all local data

    Data Backup

    • You can export your preferences and favorites
    • Backup files contain no personal information
    • Backups are stored locally and can be shared at your discretion

    Deletion of Data

    To delete your data:

    • 1. Clear app data through device settings, or
    • 2. Uninstall the app All locally stored preferences and selections will be removed.
